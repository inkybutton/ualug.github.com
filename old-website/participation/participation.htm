Calls for Participation
Installfest
If you're interested in helping out with any of the Installfests please contact the coordinator. We keep a list of volunteers to contact whenever an Installfest is being planned.
Beginner's Tutorial
If you're interested in running a tutorial or being a helper at one please contact the coordinator. We keep a list of people willing to help out and contact them whenever a tutorial is being planned.
Organisational Help
A very busy coordinator is looking for someone to share the workload. Duties would include distributing fliers on noticeboards and department offices, being first contact for selected events, and updating the website. Should be someone who's local to the city campus. please contact the coordinator and we'll sort out details.
